Recipe #1
Finger-Lickin'-Good Fried Chicken

  • Time: Ain't nothin' but a thang.
  • Serves: The whole family.
Ingredients Quantity
Love Buckets Full
Grease 2 Gallons
Chicken Mother hen
2 chicks
Time About 4 hours

Step 1: Take the legs and wings of mother hen and throw into deep frier.
Step 2: Take the dark meat of the little two hens and throw into deep frier.
Step 3: Wait for 4 hours.
Step 4: Throw away horribly burned chicken meat.
Step 5: Drive to KFC, buy a bucket of chicken + popcorn chikcen box
(being vigilant to order a side of potatoes and corn on the cog) and
enjoy... then run back to KFC and buy the same thing so that your family has something to eat.

Source: KFC Fried Chicken

Recipe #2
Momma's Sweet Simmered Pulled Pork Sliders

  • Time: All the time in the world.
  • Serves: One hungry son.
Ingredients Quantity
Racks of Ribs From 2 Baby Pig's Backs
Hawaiin Rolls 2 Packages of 5
BBQ Sauce 2 Gallons
Hefeweizen 2 six packs
Time About 4 days

Step 1: Take succulent baby back ribs and place in crock pot.
Step 2: Pour 2 gallons of BBQ Sauce into pot.
Step 3: Pour one Hefe into crock pot and suck back on the other 11
bottles as the ingredients meld for 3 days.
Step 4: Eat a package of sweet rolls while you're waiting.
(Your son won't be the wiser ;) ).
Step 5: After realizing that the crock pot wasn't turned on to simmer,
actually turn the crock pot on and let cook for 1 full day.
Step 6: Remove the pork from the bone and place 5 hefty portions
on the remaining 5 sweet rolls.
Step 7: Pray to the good Lord that your growing boy of 26 doesn't become hangry after downing his 5 sandwhiches when he visits home, demanding for more food.
(Law'd knows we don't have enough reserves in our pantry to satiate him.)

Source: Texas Pulled Pork

Recipe #3
Cousin Eddy's Dragon-Breath Baked Beans

  • Time: If you have to ask, you'll never know.
  • Serves: All the relatives, including 2nd cousins.
Ingredients Quantity
Baked Beans Clean out the grocery store aisle
Brown Sugar 2 packs
Ghost Chilis 5 whole peppers
A Bottle
of Jack 		Nuf said
Time However long it takes to cook, eat,
and drive to the hospital

Step 1: Fill a soup pot with 20 cans of baked beans.
Step 2: Carefully add two bags worth of brown sugar into pot.
Step 3: Put on Haz-Mat suit and enter into the same room as the chilis.
Step 4: Use tungsten clamps to grab bag of ghost chilis and bring into kitchen.
Step 5: After first tungsten clamps have melted, use a new set of clamps for each one that melts while handling a ghost pepper and adding it to the pot.
Step 6: Dare your littlest cousins to eat some baked beans.
Step 7: Call your wife for bail money after causing 3rd degree burns on the inside of your juvenile relatives' mouths.

Source: Spicy Baked Beans Recipe
